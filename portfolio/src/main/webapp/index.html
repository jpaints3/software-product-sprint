Hi everyone, nice to meet you! My name is Josiah and I am a sophomore at Colgate. Big mad scientist energy! XD

What is your background?
I've been in New York for practically all my life. I grew up in Yonkers, and then moved to New Rochelle in middle school. While in New Rochelle, I got into a program called Prep for Prep and it was with the help of this program that I applied and got into Phillips Exeter Academy. While at Exeter, I spent most of my time studying Latin but sometime in my senior year, I decided to make a commitment to computer science, and now here I am! :)
